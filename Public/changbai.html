ChangBai Mountains


Location Jilin Provience
Recommended Seasons Summer and Fall
Things to Do Tianchi (Heavenly Lake)    Skiing

Located near the Korean border, spectacular Eternally White Mountain is China's largest nature reserve with rare sables, tigers and wild ginseng.

Hot springs, waterfalls, Heavenly Lake, and the nature-reserve area are the top attractions in the mountains.

Among them Heavenly Lake is the most gorgeous and a must-visit place. While trekking around Heavenly Lake, you may be surprised and

overwhelmed by the scenery, which is different, but equally spectacular, under different weather conditions.
