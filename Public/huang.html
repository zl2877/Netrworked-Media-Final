HuangShan Mountain


Location Anhui Provience
Recommended Seasons Spring and Summer
Things to Do Pine and sea of clouds

Huangshan is steeped in picturesque natural landscapes and history.

These mystical and mist-ical mountains are the most beautiful and most famous in China.

Their classic attractions are grand dawns and their "four natural wonders": peculiar pines, oddly-shaped rocks, seas of clouds, and hot springs.
